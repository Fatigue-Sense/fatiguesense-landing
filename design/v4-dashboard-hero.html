What it is

Mental fatigue is the leading cause of error in high-stakes work. Pilots, surgeons, drivers, and operators face the same invisible threat: attention decay they cannot feel.

FatigueSense tracks subtle eye-movement biomarkers through a standard webcam. PERCLOS, blink rate, micro-sleep duration - turned from invisible physiology into a readable, actionable signal. No wearables, no calibration, no configuration.

How it works

Step 1

Open FatigueSense

Launch in your browser. No install, no account, no configuration needed.

Step 2

Allow webcam access

Grant camera permission once. The feed processes locally and discards in real time, frame by frame.

Step 3

Read your fatigue score

A live score and trend chart shows your cognitive state. Get alerts before fatigue compromises your attention.

Why it matters

Tracks what you cannot feel

PERCLOS, blink rate, and micro-sleep patterns reveal fatigue 15-30 minutes before you notice it yourself.

Your webcam is the only hardware

No wearables, no sensors, no headsets. If your laptop has a camera, you are ready.

Alerts before the critical moment

Configurable thresholds for early, mid, and critical fatigue. Take a break, switch tasks, or hand off.

Session history with trend analysis

Review fatigue patterns across days, compare sessions, and understand your personal cognitive rhythm.

Your face never leaves your machine.

FatigueSense runs entirely on your computer. The camera feed processes locally and discards instantly, frame by frame. No cloud uploads, no data storage, no account required. Privacy is not a feature we added. It is the architecture we chose.
